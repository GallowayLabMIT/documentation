N3 media¶

Used for neuronal differentiation during direct conversion.

  • Total volume: 500 mL

Component

%

Volume

Glutamax (1%)

1%

5 mL

N2 (100x stock)

100x stock

5 mL

B27 (50x stock)

50x stock

10 mL

Pen/Strep (1%)

1%

5 mL

FBS (optional)

2%

10 mL

DMEM F12

add to 500 mL

Add the following small molecules to small volumes immediatly before media addition, to a final volume of 10 ng/mL:

  • GDNF, from 1,000x stock (10,000 ng/ml)

  • BDNF, from 1,000x stock (10,000 ng/ml)

  • CNTF, from 1,000x stock (10,000 ng/ml)

  • FGF10, from 10,000x stock (100,000 ng/ml)

  • RepSox, if in human cells, from 1000x stock.
